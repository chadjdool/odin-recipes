Tacos

Yummy tacos!

Ingredients

  • corn tortillas
  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • onion
  • lettuce
  • ground beef
  • taco sauce

Steps

  1. cook ground beef
  2. cut up lettuce, onions, and tomatoes
  3. prepare some shredded cheese
  4. fry the tortillas
  5. add all the ingredients to the fried tortilla and enjoy!
